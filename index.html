People

Master list of people. ID, Name and Group are mandatory (Group must be one of the groups defined below). Add any extra columns (Email, Phone, etc.) to keep alongside.

Groups

Groups slice the workforce into teams (e.g. "Agent Call 1", "Agent Call 2"). Each plan in the Demand tab targets one or more groups.

Shift Types

All shift codes the team uses (e.g. C6, C7, C730). When you add a new plan in the Demand tab, every shift listed here appears as a row by default; remove the rows that don't apply to that plan.
